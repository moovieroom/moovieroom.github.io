Host Helper

So you want to host something in Cytube. Great! I can help you out with a few things.

Here's what I can do:

  • Create an announcement post for you to copy and paste to the thread, including time zone conversions.
  • Add the date for your event to the calendar above so anyone else who uses this form knows not to book something on the same day.

Unfortunately, I'm just some code and not a host. Here's what you will still need to do yourself:

  • Find or upload videos to watch in Cytube.
  • Post the announcement and reminders into the moovie thread.
  • Host the event!
